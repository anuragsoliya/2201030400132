Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Involves tensing and then relaxing different muscle groups in the body, usually starting from the toes and working up to the head.

Deep Breathing Exercises

Focuses on slow, deep breathing to help calm the mind and body. Techniques include diaphragmatic breathing and 4-7-8 breathing.

Visualization

Involves imagining a peaceful scene or setting, such as a beach or forest, and focusing on the details to promote relaxation.
